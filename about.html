About Finance Tracker

Welcome to Finance Tracker, your personal finance management tool designed to help you effortlessly track your income, manage expenses, and achieve your savings goals. Finance Tracker provides an easy-to-use platform to take control of your financial health.

Key Features

Income Tracking

Easily log and categorize your income sources to keep a clear view of your earnings.

Expense Management

Monitor your spending habits by recording your expenses in various categories.

Budget Planning

Create budgets for different spending categories and stay within your financial limits.

Savings Goals

Set and track your savings goals to achieve your financial objectives.

Reports and Analysis

Generate detailed reports to analyze your financial trends and make informed decisions.

Multi-Currency Support

Track your finances in your preferred currency for a personalized experience.
