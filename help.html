Help & Support

Frequently asked questions and support.

1. How do I track my income?

Your income can be tracked by navigating to the Income Tracker page and entering your income source and amount.

2. How do I add an expense?

To add an expense, go to the Expense Tracker page and fill out the expense form with the necessary details.

3. Can I set a budget?

Yes! You can set a budget in the Budget Overview section where you can view and manage your budget effectively.

4. How do I contact support?

You can reach out to our support team via the Contact page. We're here to help!
